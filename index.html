What's inside
EVERYTHING IN
ONE APP
Movies & TV Series
Vast library of films and complete TV series with detailed metadata, ratings, and high-quality playback.
Live TV (IPTV)
Stream live television channels worldwide directly inside the app — no external player needed.
Anime
Full anime catalog with subbed and dubbed episodes, season tracking, and complete watch history.
Sports
Watch live sports from around the world — football, cricket, tennis, and more in real time.
Minigames
Built-in minigames to keep you entertained between episodes — exclusive to Drishya.
Dubs & Multi-Audio
Switch between dubbed and subbed audio instantly. Wide language support across all content.
Premium Playback
Hardware-accelerated player via media_kit with advanced buffering and subtitle rendering.
Smart Search
Blazing-fast search across all content types with smart suggestions and instant results.
Watch History
Automatically tracks everything you watch and lets you resume exactly where you left off.
Dynamic Colors
Android 12+ Monet theming adapts the entire UI to your wallpaper palette automatically.
Material 3 UI
Glassmorphism and Material You design with fluid animations powered by flutter_animate.
Subtitles
Multi-language subtitle support with customizable font size, style, and on-screen position.
