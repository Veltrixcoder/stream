What's included
EVERYTHING IN ONE APP
Movies & TV Series
A vast library of films and complete TV series with detailed metadata, ratings, and seamless high-quality playback.
Live TV (IPTV)
Stream live television channels worldwide directly inside the app — no external player or setup needed.
Anime
Full anime catalog with subbed and dubbed episodes, season tracking, and complete watch history.
Sports
Watch live sports events from around the world — football, cricket, tennis, and more in real time.
Minigames
Built-in minigames to keep you entertained between episodes — a unique feature exclusive to Drishya.
Dubs & Multi-Audio
Switch between dubbed and subbed audio tracks instantly. Wide language support across all content types.
Premium Playback
Hardware-accelerated player via media_kit with advanced buffering optimizations and subtitle rendering.
Smart Search
Blazing-fast search across all content types with smart suggestions and instant results.
Watch History
Automatically tracks everything you watch and lets you resume exactly where you left off.
Dynamic Colors
Android 12+ Monet theming that adapts the entire UI to your wallpaper palette automatically.
Modern UI
Glassmorphism and Material 3 design elements with fluid animations powered by flutter_animate.
Subtitles
Multi-language subtitle support with customizable font size, style, and on-screen positioning.
