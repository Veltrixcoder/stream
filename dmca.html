Important Notice About Content
Drishya does not host, store, or distribute any movies, TV shows, or media files on its own servers. All content accessible through the app is provided by non-affiliated, independent third-party providers. Drishya is a free, open-source Android application that acts solely as a client interface.
01
Important Notice

Drishya is an open-source Android streaming client built with Flutter. It provides a user interface that connects to external streaming providers configured by the user. This website (drishya.app) serves as the official landing page, APK download hub, and provider configuration portal for the app.

No Content Hosting
Neither this website nor the Drishya Android application hosts, stores, caches, or distributes any movies, TV shows, live streams, anime episodes, or sports broadcasts on our servers or infrastructure.
Third-Party Providers Only
All media content streamed through Drishya originates exclusively from non-affiliated third-party public providers. We have no control over, affiliation with, or responsibility for said providers or their content.
Open-Source & MIT Licensed
Drishya's source code is fully open and available at github.com/Shashwat-CODING/Drishya under the MIT License. You are free to inspect, fork, and audit the entire codebase.
